Make coffee
Open Keurig coffee maker to expose reusable k-cup.  Empty contents of k-cup in trash.  Scoop fresh coffee grounds into reusable k-cup.  Place back into Keurig coffee maker and close top.  Put mug on platform of machine.  Press largest coffee size button on machine.  When finished brewing, add desired sweeteners.
