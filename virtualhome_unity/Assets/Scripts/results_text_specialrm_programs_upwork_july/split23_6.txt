Polish your shoes
Get a shoe polish kit and shoes that can be shined and sit down.  Open the kit, apply polish, rub off, and shine with shammy.
[Walk] <BEDROOM> (1)
[Find] <SHOE-SHINE KIT> (1)
[Grab] <SHOE-SHINE KIT> (1)
[Grab] <SHOES> (1)
[Walk] <BED> (1)
[Sit] <BED> (1)
[Grab] <BRUSH> (1)
[PutObjBack] <BRUSH> (1)
[Grab] <RAG> (1)
[PutObjBack] <RAG> (1)
[PutObjBack] <SHOE-SHINE KIT> (1)
