Face washing
Wet face with water. Put face soap on hands and work into a lather. Apply lather to face and scrub until clean. Wash soap off with water.
[Walk] <BATHROOM> (1)
[TurnTo] <SINK> (1)
[SwitchOn] <FAUCET> (1)
[Pour] <WATER> (1) <HANDS> (1)
[Wash] <FACE> (1)
[Grab] <FACE SOAP> (1)
[Pour] <FACE SOAP> (1) <HANDS> (1)
[PutObjBack] <FACE SOAP> (1)
[Spread] <FACE SOAP> (1) <FACE> (1)
[Scrub] <FACE> (1)
[Pour] <WATER> (1) <HANDS> (1)
[Rinse] <FACE> (1)
[SwitchOff] <FAUCET> (1)
