Shave with an electric shaver.
Pick up razor.  Push the button to turn it on.  Move it in a linear motion against the beard growth.  Make sure to get the bottom of your chin, your cheeks, and mustache area.
[Walk] <BATHROOM> (1)
[Find] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[Grab] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[PlugIn] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[SwitchOn] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[Shave] <FACE> (1)
[SwitchOff] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[PlugOut] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
