Wash hands
Go to the bathroom. Open faucet. Wet hands. Grab soap. Scrub hands with soap. Rinse hands with water. Close faucet. Grab towel. Dry hands with towel.
[SwitchOn] <FAUCET> (1)
[Grab] <SOAP> (1)
[Wash] <HANDS> (1)
[Scrub] <HANDS> (1)
[PutObjBack] <SOAP> (1)
[Rinse] <HANDS> (1)
[SwitchOff] <FAUCET> (1)
[Grab] <TOWEL> (1)
[Wipe] <HANDS> (1)
[PutObjBack] <TOWEL> (1)
