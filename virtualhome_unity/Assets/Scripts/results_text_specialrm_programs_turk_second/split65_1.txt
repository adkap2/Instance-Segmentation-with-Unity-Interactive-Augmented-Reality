Fix a sandwich
Take bread out of a cabinet. Open fridge and take out sandwich meat, cheese, mayo, and mustard. Open a cabinet and take out a knife. Spread mayo and mustard on two pieces of bread. Put sandwich meat and cheese on the bread. Put two halves together to make a whole sandwich on a plate. Put away sandwich meat, cheese, mayo, mustard, and bread. Clean knife and counters.
