Sewing pillows
Cut fabric desired size using cutting mat and rotary cutter.  Using sewing machine, sew around fabric, right sides together, leaving an opening for turning and inserting pillow forms.  Turn fabric right side out and iron, taking care to not burn fabric.  Insert pillow form and smooth fabric.  With needle and thread, hand sew opening closed and snip any excess thread with scissors.
