Make coffee
Pour 2% reduced milk in the mug, put 1 1/2 tsp sugar in the mug, put the mug in microwave for express 1 minute, take out mug from the microwave and put 1 tsp leveled instant coffee in the mug, whisk with fork or small whisk, serve
