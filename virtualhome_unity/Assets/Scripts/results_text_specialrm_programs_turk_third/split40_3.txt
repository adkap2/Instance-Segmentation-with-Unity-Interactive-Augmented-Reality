Shampoo hair
Remove clothing and turn faucet of shower to desired water temperature. Enter shower. Rinse hair under shower head until soaked through. Grasp bottle of shampoo and open cap. Pour a fairly generous amount into unused hand. Replace bottle to original location. Lather shampoo in hands for a couple seconds. Place both hands on head and begin to work shampoo into hair. Continue until hair is completely lathered. Beginning from the top of head, rinse hair under shower head. Continue until all shampoo is removed from hair by the water.
