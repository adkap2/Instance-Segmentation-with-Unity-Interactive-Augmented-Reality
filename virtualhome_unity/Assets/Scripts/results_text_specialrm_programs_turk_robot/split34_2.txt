Shave my head
Shave my head with a 1/8 adapter on an clippers.
[Walk] <BATHROOM> (1)
[Find] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[Grab] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[PlugIn] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[SwitchOn] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[Shave] <HAIR> (1)
[Shave] <HAIR> (1)
[Shave] <HAIR> (1)
[Shave] <HAIR> (1)
[SwitchOff] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[PlugOut] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
[PutObjBack] <ELECTRIC SHAVER> (1)
